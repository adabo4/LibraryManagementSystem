Navigating the Library Management System

Titles

This section encompasses all titles that your library offers for borrowing. Among the titles, you will find various types of media such as books and DVDs. This section is essential for orienting yourself in the available materials and finding information about individual titles.

Books: This subsection contains all available books in your library. Here, you'll find a variety of literary works, fiction, and non-fiction literature.

DVD: This subsection includes all available DVD titles. Among these titles, you will find movies, documentaries, and other audiovisual content.

Members

This section deals with information about library members. It allows for managing member records and provides various options such as adding new members or browsing the list of all members.

All Members: This subsection displays a comprehensive list of all registered library members. It serves as a useful tool for monitoring and managing library users.

Add Member: This subsection offers the possibility to add a new member to the library. Using this tool, you can record a new member along with their relevant details.

Rentals

This section focuses on the rental process and management of library materials. It offers various options for managing library rentals and tracking the progress of rentals.

All Rentals: This subsection shows a list of all current library rentals. It provides an overview of who has borrowed what.

Rent a Title: This subsection allows library members to borrow a specific title. It facilitates searching and selecting materials for borrowing.

Rentals Past Due: This subsection informs about rentals that are overdue. It provides a quick way to search and manage overdue rentals.

Queue of Requests: This subsection deals with managing the queue of rental requests. It allows tracking which materials are requested and in what order.

Notifications

This final section informs library members about important notifications and messages related to the library's functioning. Here, members can find current information and alerts from the library.
